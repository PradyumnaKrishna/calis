Recommended brand direction

Calis

A quiet, adult, warm-neutral identity. The product should feel composed and private: soft off-white in light mode, near-black in dark mode, restrained contrast, and no decorative accent colors unless a workflow truly needs status feedback.

Light mode

Calis

Minimal, focused, grown-up.

Use neutral surfaces, clear typography, and calm spacing. Avoid loud highlights, playful gradients, and oversized decorative UI.

Token set

  • Surface: Sand 3 light, Sand 3 dark

  • Primary text: Sand 12 light, Sand 12 dark

Brand behavior

Keep the wordmark simple, text-based, and confident. Use “Calis” in title case, generous whitespace, and neutral surfaces. Let content and interaction quality carry the brand.

Minimal semantic colors

These colors are not brand decoration. Use them only for state: destructive actions, confirmations, caution, and keyboard focus or selected controls.
